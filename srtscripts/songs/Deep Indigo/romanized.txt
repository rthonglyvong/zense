asai shoogo kookashita, kannijoo,
nekorobu mama
hakushi no jinsei ni 
hakushu no oto ga hitotsu natte iru
karappona jibun o kyoo mo utatte ita
 
kawaranai yoo ni
kimi ga shuyaku no purotto o kaku 
nooto no naka
tomatta gasu suidō 
seken mo nyūsu mo shosentanin koto
kono jinsei sae hora, 
inku mitaida
 
ano koro zutto atama ni kaita yume mo otona ni naru hodo 
jikoo ni natte iku
 
tada, tada kumo o miagete mo
shikai wa kyoo mo nagareru mama
tooku aoida yoru ni hana oyogu
haru to mimagau hodo ni
kimi o tada miushinau yoo ni
 
korobanai yooni shita o muita
jinsei wa doonimo 
dakyoo de deki teru
kokoro mo unmei mo rabusongu mo jinsei mo shinjinai
shosen urenai nara zenbu ga muda da
 
wazato koboshita yume de egaita 
ima ni nesobetta mama de 
jikō o matteiru
 
tada, tada mafuta no uragawa
tooku egaku kimi o mita mama
nooto, usui yoru sumi ni hana oyogu
boku no me ni matahitotsu
 
jinsei wa dakyoo no renzoku nan da
sonna koto tooni wakattetan da
eruma, kimi nan da yoo
kimi dake ga boku no ongaku nan da
 
kono uta wa ato hachi juuji
jinsei no kachi wa 
owarikata darou kara
 
tada, tada kimi dake o egaku
shikai no ai mo nijinda mama
tooku aoida sora ni hana oyogu
kono me oou ai ni joo
 
tada, tada
tooku aoida sora, kimi ga suzumu
tada yoru o oyogu yoo ni
